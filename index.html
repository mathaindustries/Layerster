Surface Preparation

Our meticulous surface preparation is the cornerstone of a durable and flawless finish. We offer:

  • Cleaning and Degreasing: Removing oils, dirt, and residues.
  • Sandblasting: Abrasive blasting to eliminate rust, old coatings, and imperfections.
  • Chemical Pretreatment: Applying conversion coatings like phosphates to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance.

Precision Powder Application

Using cutting-edge technology, we apply powder coatings with precision and consistency:

  • Electrostatic Spraying: Our advanced electrostatic spray guns ensure even distribution of powder, adhering uniformly to every surface.
  • Custom Coating Options: From standard colors to custom-matched shades, we offer a broad spectrum of colors and finishes, including matte, gloss, and textured.

Professional Curing

Our controlled curing process ensures a durable, high-quality finish:

  • Temperature-Controlled Ovens: We cure the coated parts in specialized ovens, where the powder melts and forms a tough, continuous film.
  • Quality Assurance: We monitor curing times and temperatures meticulously to achieve optimal results.

Rigorous Quality Control

We adhere to stringent quality standards to ensure the excellence of our coatings:

  • Visual and Physical Inspections: Checking for uniformity, coverage, and surface defects.
  • Thickness and Adhesion Testing: Measuring coating thickness and performing adhesion tests to ensure durability and performance.
