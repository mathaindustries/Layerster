Our Story

We started our business in 2024, specialized in Powder Coating in the name of Layerster Private Limited. We handle jobs and orders from India and Overseas and can deliver finished components and parts as per requirement and quality standards. The entire range we offer is formulated using premium quality material and highly advanced machines. We offer products to our valuable customer at reasonable rates. Our coating provide optimum choice in appearance, durability, cost and environmental impact. Our services can be custom made in accordance to the customer specifications.

Vision

  • We focus on providing innovation, quality, customization, customer focus, visual impact and continuous improvement.
  • We prioritize on providing efficiency and productivity and aims to be the best and world class powder coating company

MISSION

  • Our Mission is to deliver superior coatings that exceed customer expectations and ensuring long lasting protection and to provide high quality powder coating products and solution that enhance the durability, appearance and performance of products
  • We aim to be the preferred partner for powder coating needs, contributing to the success and satisfaction of our clients
